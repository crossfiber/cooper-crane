The fleet runs from a 2-ton mini spider crane that fits through a standard gate up to a 40-ton mobile crane, with 17-ton and 35-ton machines in between, plus boom trucks and accessories.
Two yards cover six counties. The Southeast yard serves Broward, Miami-Dade and Palm Beach. The Southwest yard serves Lee, Collier and Charlotte.
Yes. Every crane comes with a certified, experienced Cooper Crane operator who handles the rigging and signaling and treats your site and crew with courtesy and respect.
Tell us three things: how heavy the load is, how high it has to go and how far the crane has to reach, plus any access limits. We will spec the right machine. If you are not sure, a quick call sorts it out.
Earlier is better for scheduling, but we keep multiple machines at each yard and will always try to work in urgent and same-week lifts. Call the yard nearest your job and we will find a slot.
Every crane is run by a Cooper Crane operator. We bring the machine and run it; you point at what needs to move. No separate operator to hire.
Common lifts include rooftop AC units, roof trusses, roofing material, generators and pest-control tarps, plus plenty of custom one-off lifts like trees, steel, signage and hot tubs.
We invoice through QuickBooks and accept credit card, Cash App and Zelle.
